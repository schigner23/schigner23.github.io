Diabetes Information for Children Born with Type 1

New parents who are figuring out their children are born with a lifelong dieases can be overwhelming. This foundation gives support, moral, financial, and health wise to these new families. They devote, time energy and resources to families going through this for the first time.

Disbetes Information for Children Who Were Diagnosed with Type 2 Later in Life

This can be a hard transition for children who have not had to deal with it their whole lives. It has often been said that if you are able to start the routine early in life it becomes second nature. When a child or teen has to change their routines in such a drastic way problems can arise with explaining the importance of ensuring monitoring blood sugar. This foundation works with doctors and certified practitioners who specialize in diabetes to help families learn how to handle dibetes together. They also provide financial support to those who may not beable to afford insulin for their children.
